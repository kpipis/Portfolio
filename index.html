General Information

I'm an Electrical Engineer and Computer Engineer, working in the area of Power Systems. My doctoral thesis was on eddy current Non Destructive Control modeling.

My interests focus on Electromagnetics, Remote Sensing of the Environment and Power Systems. Lately, I have found strong interest in coding with HTML, CSS and Javascript.

I currently work at the Helleninc Electricity Distribution Network Operator, where I am in charge of the specifications and the technical evaluation of the materials of Aerial Networks and Substations. I am also lecturer at the University of Thessaly teaching Control Systems, Industrial Automations and Power Systems Control and Stability and at the School of Pedagogical and Technological Education (ASPETE), Athens, where I teach the laboratory part of the Power Systems course.
